REFUND POLICY

Non-Refundable Situations

We cannot provide a refund if your situation falls under any of the categories below. These events are outside our control and occur within the hospital's own administrative or medical processes.

  • Hospital-side issues (delays, system errors, doctor schedule changes, additional tests required)
  • Insufficient preparation by the customer
  • The customer did not follow the provided instructions
  • Incorrect or incomplete questionnaire
  • Fasting failure or consuming restricted foods
  • Schedule mistakes made by the customer
  • Delayed results caused by the hospital
  • Late submission of required information by the customer
  • Events naturally possible within medical systems (unexpected workflow delays, internal processing times, hospital protocols)

A full refund is issued only when the mistake is clearly on our side.
